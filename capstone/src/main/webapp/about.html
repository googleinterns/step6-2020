The Covid-19 Pandemic

Is an ongoing global pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019. Many people has been affected, including local businesses. Many businesses were forced to pause their business to help slow the spread of the pandemic.
“I never could have imagined being closed for days and days,” says Barb Skupien, the 51-year-old owner of Embellish, a jewelry boutique in Asheville, North Carolina that she first opened in 2015 after previously running a similar store in Chicago for roughly seven years. As it stands, she believes she has enough money saved to “string it along for a couple of months,” she says.

We created this platform to bring the community together

As a group of three Google STEP interns, we decided to build a platform that allows businesses to tell their pandemic story and local people to help out in any way they can.

Together, we can all help!

There's many ways you can help by using this platform.
1) You can share your local businesses' profiles to your friends and family.
2) You can leave a kind and supportive comment.
3) Educate yourselves on what's happening in your local or global community.

About Us

Support Local Businesses is a platform that gives local businesses the opportunity to share their pandemic story, voice out their struggles, promote their business, and reach out to their local community for help. The general people can use this platform to educate themselves with real stories from their local or gloabl businesses that are being impacted from COVID-19. There are several ways to help and build your local community digitally. You can educate yourselves, share their business profiles with friends and family, or just leave kind and supportive comments to help them get through it!

As a team of three Google STEP interns, we decided to build this platform during the COVID-19 pandemic because we feel that this is what's helpful for those local communities that are affected. You can read more about the pandemic and keep updated at the CDC website.
